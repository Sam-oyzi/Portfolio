Intro

What I am all about.

I'm Sam Houzi, Passionate about the railway industry and technological innovation, I excel as a BIM Modeler for Catenary Studies. With significant experience, I possess deep expertise in the technical aspects of catenary studies, 3D modeling, and automation using Design Script and Python. Additionally, I create augmented reality experiences tailored to the railway sector, further enhancing project visualization and interaction.

With over six years of experience in catenary studies and 3D BIM modeling, I have specialized in high-voltage catenary studies (25,000V, 3,000V, 1,500V, LAC), using advanced tools such as Autocad, Revit, Inventor, Blender, and UnrealEngine5.
